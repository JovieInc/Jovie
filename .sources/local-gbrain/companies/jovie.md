# Jovie Technology Inc.

## Overview

**Jovie** is an AI-powered artist workspace for independent musicians, artists, and their teams. Founded in **2024** by **Tim White**, the company is headquartered in Los Angeles, CA.

## Product

Jovie is designed as "your always-on AI artist manager":

- **Release OS** — Each release gets its own smart page with fan actions, presave countdowns, listen links, subscribe/follow buttons
- **Fan Paths** — Conversion-optimized artist profile pages that capture listeners into the artist's ecosystem
- **Pre-save Campaigns** — Built-in presave functionality with countdown widgets
- **Pitch Drafts** — AI-assisted drafting of pitches for playlists, blogs, sync licensing
- **Media Library** — Organized asset management for releases (artwork, masters, press kits)
- **Artist Profiles** — Smart link-in-bio pages that convert visitors to fans/funders
