# Ryan Donovan

Music producer and songwriter based in Los Angeles. Co-writer and co-producer on multiple tracks with Tim White.

## Collaborations with Tim White
- "This Is Love" (2020) — Tim White & Ryan Donovan
- "Doge (to Mars)" (2020) — Ryan Donovan credited
- "Supernova Girl" (2020) — Tim White & Ryan Donovan
- "Tomorrow Sounds" (2020) — Tim White & Ryan Donovan

## Note
Ryan has asked to have his name removed as credited artist on Spotify and other streaming platforms. He remains a writer and producer but is no longer publicly credited as an artist. He should be listed as "Ryan Donovan" in writing/production credits only (not as primary or featured artist).
