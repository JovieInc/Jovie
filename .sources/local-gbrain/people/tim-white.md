# Tim White

## Biography

Tim White is a DJ, producer, singer-songwriter, and tech founder based in **West Hollywood, Los Angeles**, with over **15 years** in the electronic music scene and a growing footprint in **AI/agent tooling**.

Born April 5, 1991 in New Jersey, Tim moved to LA and built a career spanning music production, DJing, release management, and artist development. In 2024, he founded **[[companies/jovie|Jovie Technology Inc.]]** — an AI-powered artist workspace.
