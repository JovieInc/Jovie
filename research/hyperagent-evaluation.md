# HyperAgent — Research & Recommendation

## What Is HyperAgent?

**HyperAgent** is a cloud-based AI agent platform built by **the team at Airtable** (Formagrid Inc.). It is a **separate product** from Airtable itself.

**Key facts:**
- **Operator:** Formagrid Inc. (Airtable's corporate entity)
- **Founder/CEO:** Howie Liu, co-founder and CEO of Airtable
- **Launch:** Announced ~late February 2026, Founding 500 program announced May 22, 2026
- **Real site:** hyperagent.com (hyperagent.ai was parked/for sale — NOT them)

## What Does It Do?

Production-grade cloud platform for building and deploying autonomous AI agents. Each agent gets:
- Full isolated computing environment (real VM per session)
- Browser automation
- Image, video, and audio generation
- Hundreds of enterprise integrations via in-chat OAuth
- Data warehouse access, geolocation/mapping
- Deep research capabilities
- Skills/memory accumulation across sessions
- Deployment to Slack and other channels

## The $20K Credits — Founding 500 Program

- **$10 million total** in inference credits committed by Airtable
- **500 founders** selected for the "Founding 500" cohort
- **$20,000 in inference credits per founder**
- Also: first 1,000 signups get $1,000 bonus credits + 2.5x cost subsidy on frontier models

## Fundraising Agent Use Case

**Possible but NOT purpose-built.** HyperAgent is a general-purpose agent platform. Could be configured for:
- Prospect research on investors
- Personalized outreach at scale
- CRM integration
- Fundraising metrics analysis
- Competitive analysis

**But:** No evidence of being marketed specifically as a "fundraising agent." For dedicated fundraising AI, also evaluate Blackbaud Development Agent or Salesforce Agentforce.

## Connection to Every.to / Dan Shipper

**None found.** Dan Shipper (CEO of Every) and HyperAgent are separate entities.
