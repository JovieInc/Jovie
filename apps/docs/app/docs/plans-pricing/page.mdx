# Plans & Pricing

Jovie offers three plans designed for artists at every stage. New accounts start with a 14-day Pro trial — no credit card required.

## Free

**$0/month — Free forever**

Everything you need to get started with a smart artist profile and basic analytics.

**Includes:**
- Unlimited smart links
- Auto-sync from Spotify
- Smart deep links to native apps
- Edit & customize smart links
- Release pages with listen links per DSP
- Short link redirects
- Vanity URLs
- Auto DSP detection & linking
- Manual release creation
- Public artist profile page
- Artist bio & social links
- Subscribe / follow page
- Contact page
- About page
- Tour dates (Bandsintown)
- Click & visit tracking
- Basic analytics (30 days)
- Audience intelligence
- Up to 100 contacts
- AI assistant (10 messages/day)
- 1 AI pitch generation per release

## Pro

**$39/month or $375/year**

For growing artists who want to own their audience and optimize their reach.

**Everything in Free, plus:**
- Release notifications to fans
- Pre-save campaigns
- Pre-release & countdown pages
- Extended analytics (180 days)
- Advanced analytics & geographic insights
- Traffic quality filtering
- AI-powered insights
- Unlimited contacts
- Contact export
- Fan CRM
- Tips & payments
- Earnings dashboard
- URL encryption
- Ad pixel tracking (Facebook, Google, TikTok)
- Verified badge
- AI assistant (100 messages/day)
- AI pitch generation (5 per release)
- Priority support

## Max

**$149/month or $1,430/year**

For serious artists who want full automation and advanced ops.

**Everything in Pro, plus:**
- Release plan generation
- Metadata submission agent
- Unlimited analytics
- AI assistant (500 messages/day)
- Unlimited AI pitch generation
- Stripe Connect payouts
- Fan subscriptions
- Email campaigns
- API access
- Team management
- Webhooks
- White-label / custom domain
- A/B testing
