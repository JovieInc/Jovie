# Plans & Pricing

Jovie offers three plans designed for artists at every stage. Start free, upgrade when you need more.

## Free

**$0/month -- Free forever**

Everything you need to get started with a smart artist profile and basic analytics.

**Includes:**
- Unlimited smart links
- Auto-sync from Spotify
- Public profile with bio, social links, and tour dates
- Smart deep links to native apps
- Release pages with listen links per DSP
- "Use This Sound" pages for influencer sharing
- Subscribe, contact, and about pages
- Release notifications to fans
- Click & visit tracking (30-day retention)
- Audience intelligence (up to 100 contacts)
- AI assistant (25 messages/day)

## Founding Member

**$12/month -- Limited time, locked in for life**

All Pro features at early supporter pricing. This rate never increases.

## Pro

**$39/month or $348/year**

For growing artists who want to own their audience and optimize their reach.

**Everything in Free, plus:**
- Pre-release & countdown pages
- Remove Jovie branding
- Extended analytics (90-day retention)
- Advanced analytics & geographic insights
- Filter your own visits from analytics
- AI-powered insights
- Unlimited contacts
- Contact export (CSV)
- Fan CRM
- Tips & payments
- Earnings dashboard
- Ad pixel tracking (Facebook, Google, TikTok)
- Verified badge
- AI assistant (100 messages/day)
- Priority support

## Growth

**$99/month or $948/year**

For serious artists with high-volume needs.

**Everything in Pro, plus:**
- Full analytics (1-year retention)
- AI assistant (500 messages/day)
- A/B testing (Coming soon)
- Automated follow-ups (Coming soon)
- Catalog monitoring (Coming soon)
