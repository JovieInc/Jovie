# Getting Started

Jovie’s fastest path to a live artist page is Spotify-first. Start on the
homepage, sign up, connect your Spotify artist profile, wait for your first
release to appear, then polish the page you will share with fans.

## Launch checklist

1. **Sign up** -- Start at [jov.ie](https://jov.ie) and create your account.
2. **Claim your handle** -- Pick the `jov.ie/{username}` URL you want fans to use.
3. **Connect Spotify** -- Paste your Spotify artist link during onboarding so Jovie can import your public catalog.
4. **Wait for your first release** -- Onboarding is not complete until at least one visible release is ready on your profile.
5. **Open your live profile** -- As soon as onboarding finishes, your public profile is already live.
6. **Edit your profile** -- Update your display name, photo, location, and career highlights from the dashboard.
7. **Share your profile and subscribe link** -- Send fans to `jov.ie/{username}` and `jov.ie/{username}/subscribe`.

## What fans get

- **Music immediately** -- Your profile highlights your imported release catalog and listen surfaces.
- **A live artist page** -- Fans can visit `jov.ie/{username}` as soon as onboarding completes.
- **Email signup** -- Fans can subscribe for updates from your public profile or at `/{username}/subscribe`.
