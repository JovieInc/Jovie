# Getting Started

## What is Jovie?

Jovie is the all-in-one platform for independent musicians. It provides
everything you need to manage your career, from a professional profile and
smart links for your releases to audience management, fan chat, tipping, and
in-depth analytics.

## Quickstart

1. **Sign up** -- Create your account at [jov.ie](https://jov.ie).
2. **Claim your handle** -- Choose a unique handle that fans will use to find you.
3. **Connect your DSPs** -- Link Spotify, Apple Music, and other platforms so Jovie can pull in your catalog automatically.
4. **Set up your profile** -- Add your bio, photos, and social links.
5. **Share your link** -- Start sending fans to your Jovie page.

Once you have completed these steps you are ready to explore the full feature set.
Head over to the [Features](/docs/features) section to learn more.
