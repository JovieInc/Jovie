# Guides

Step-by-step guides to help you set up and get the most out of Jovie.

## Getting started

- [Claim Your Handle](/docs/self-serve-guide/claim-handle) -- Reserve your unique artist URL.
- [Connect DSPs](/docs/self-serve-guide/connect-dsps) -- Link your streaming platforms to auto-import your catalog.
- [Set Up Your Profile](/docs/self-serve-guide/set-up-profile) -- Build your public artist page with photos, bio, and social links.

## Feature setup

- [Share Your First Smart Link](/docs/self-serve-guide/share-first-link) -- Get your music in front of fans with a single link.
- [Set Up Tipping](/docs/self-serve-guide/set-up-tipping) -- Start accepting tips from fans on your profile.
- [Set Up Ad Pixels](/docs/self-serve-guide/set-up-ad-pixels) -- Install tracking pixels for retargeting campaigns.
- [Connect Bandsintown](/docs/self-serve-guide/connect-bandsintown) -- Sync your tour dates to your Jovie profile.
