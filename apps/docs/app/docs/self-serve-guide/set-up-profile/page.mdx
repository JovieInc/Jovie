# Set Up Your Profile

A complete profile helps fans discover and connect with you. Here is how to
make the most of your Jovie page.

## Basic information

1. **Profile photo** -- Upload a high-quality photo (at least 400x400px).
2. **Display name** -- Your artist or band name as you want it shown publicly.
3. **Bio** -- A short description of who you are and what you do.

## Social links

Add links to your social media accounts so fans can follow you everywhere.
Supported platforms include Instagram, TikTok, X (Twitter), Facebook, YouTube,
and more.

## Featured content

Choose which releases, playlists, or content to feature prominently on your
profile. Featured items appear at the top of your page.

## Publishing

Once you are happy with your profile, toggle "Public" in your settings to
make it visible. You can update your profile at any time from the dashboard.
