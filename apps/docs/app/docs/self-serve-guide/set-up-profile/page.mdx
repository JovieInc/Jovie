# Set Up Your Profile

Your profile is already live when Spotify-first onboarding finishes. This step
is about polishing what fans see, not flipping a second publish switch.

## What to edit first

1. **Profile photo** -- Upload a sharp square image so your page looks finished everywhere it appears.
2. **Display name** -- Your artist or band name as you want it shown publicly.
3. **Username** -- Keep your public URL clean and easy to share.
4. **Current location and hometown** -- Give fans and industry visitors the right context.
5. **Career highlights** -- Add the milestones, press, playlist history, or traction you want Jovie to use.
6. **Target playlists** -- Set your default playlist targets for release workflows.

## What carries over automatically

- **Your handle** comes from onboarding.
- **Your public profile** is already reachable at `jov.ie/{username}`.
- **Your first release** should already be visible before onboarding completes.

## Keep improving after launch

- Add or adjust your social links so fans can follow you everywhere.
- Review how your latest release appears on the public profile.
- Test the subscribe flow from `jov.ie/{username}` or `jov.ie/{username}/subscribe`.
- Revisit this page whenever your brand, location, or story changes.
