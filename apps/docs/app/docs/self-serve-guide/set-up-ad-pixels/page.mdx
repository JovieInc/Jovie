# Set Up Ad Pixels

Install tracking pixels to retarget your Jovie visitors on Facebook, Google, and TikTok.

## Requirements

- A Pro or Growth plan
- An ad account on at least one supported platform

## Steps

### Facebook Pixel

1. Go to [Meta Events Manager](https://business.facebook.com/events_manager) and create a pixel (or find your existing one).
2. Copy your **Pixel ID** (a number like `123456789012345`).
3. In Jovie, go to **Settings > Ad Pixels**.
4. Paste your Facebook Pixel ID and save.
5. Jovie will fire page view and click events on all your public pages.

### Google Analytics

1. Go to your Google Analytics property and find your **Measurement ID** (starts with `G-`).
2. In Jovie, go to **Settings > Ad Pixels**.
3. Paste your Google Analytics Measurement ID and save.

### TikTok Pixel

1. Go to TikTok Ads Manager and create a pixel.
2. Copy your **Pixel ID**.
3. In Jovie, go to **Settings > Ad Pixels**.
4. Paste your TikTok Pixel ID and save.

## Verification

After setup, visit your Jovie profile in a browser and check your ad platform's event debugger to confirm events are firing.

## What gets tracked

- Page views on your profile and release pages
- Link clicks (which platform, which release)
- Form submissions (subscribe, contact)
- Tip events
