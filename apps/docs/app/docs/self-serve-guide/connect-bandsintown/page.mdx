# Connect Bandsintown

Sync your tour dates from Bandsintown to your Jovie profile automatically.

## Requirements

- A Bandsintown artist account with upcoming events listed

## Steps

1. Go to **Settings > Touring** in your Jovie dashboard.
2. Enter your **Bandsintown artist name** exactly as it appears on Bandsintown.
3. Save your settings.
4. Jovie syncs your tour dates automatically. New dates appear on your profile within minutes.

## What shows up

Your tour page at `jov.ie/{username}/tour` displays:

- **Venue name** -- Where the show is happening.
- **City** -- Location of the venue.
- **Date and time** -- When the show starts.
- **Ticket link** -- Direct link to buy tickets.

## Keeping it updated

Tour dates sync automatically from Bandsintown. When you add, update, or cancel a show on Bandsintown, the changes appear on your Jovie profile automatically.

## Troubleshooting

- **No dates showing?** -- Make sure your Bandsintown artist name matches exactly. Check capitalization and spelling.
- **Missing dates?** -- Verify the events are published and visible on your Bandsintown profile.
- **Wrong dates?** -- Check the timezone settings on your Bandsintown events.
