# Claim Your Handle

Your handle is your unique identifier on Jovie. It determines your public URL
(e.g., `jovie.com/yourname`) and is how fans find you on the platform.

## Steps

1. **Navigate to Settings** -- From your dashboard, go to Settings then Profile.
2. **Enter your desired handle** -- Type the handle you want. Jovie will check
   availability in real time.
3. **Confirm** -- Click "Claim Handle" to lock it in.

## Guidelines

- Handles must be between 3 and 30 characters.
- Only letters, numbers, hyphens, and underscores are allowed.
- Handles are case-insensitive (`YourName` and `yourname` are the same).
- Once claimed, a handle cannot be transferred. Contact support if you need
  to change it.
