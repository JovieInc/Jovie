# Share Your First Smart Link

Once your releases are imported, each one has a smart link ready to share. Here's how to get started.

## Find your smart links

1. Go to your **Dashboard > Links**.
2. You'll see a smart link for every release in your catalog.
3. Click any release to see its smart link URL.

## Where to share

- **Instagram bio** -- Replace your current link-in-bio with your Jovie profile URL (`jov.ie/{username}`). Every release is one tap away.
- **Twitter / X** -- Drop your smart link in a tweet when you release new music.
- **TikTok bio** -- Link to your profile or a specific release.
- **Email newsletters** -- Include smart links in fan emails for direct streaming access.
- **Live shows** -- Display your QR code at the merch table or on screen.

## Track performance

After sharing, check your analytics dashboard to see:

- How many clicks each smart link gets
- Which platforms fans prefer
- Where your traffic comes from

## Share "Use This Sound" links

Want influencers to use your track in their content? Share the sounds link instead:
`jov.ie/{username}/{release-slug}/sounds`

This page shows TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts buttons for creating content with your audio.
