# Set Up Tipping

Accept tips from fans directly on your Jovie profile. Here's how to get started.

## Requirements

- A Pro or Growth plan
- A Venmo account

## Steps

1. **Upgrade to Pro** -- Go to Settings > Billing and choose a Pro or Growth plan.
2. **Connect Venmo** -- In your dashboard settings, connect your Venmo account for payouts.
3. **Verify setup** -- Visit your tip page at `jov.ie/{username}/tip` to confirm it's working.
4. **Share your tip link** -- Add it to your social bios, share at live shows, or include it in your profile.

## Tips for maximizing tips

- **QR codes at shows** -- Display your QR code at the merch table. When fans scan it, Jovie opens the fastest tip flow.
- **Post-show sharing** -- Share your tip link on social media after a great show while the energy is high.
- **Thank your tippers** -- Check your earnings dashboard for fan messages and show appreciation.

## Tracking earnings

Visit **Dashboard > Earnings** to see:

- Total tips received
- Individual transaction history
- Payout status and timing
