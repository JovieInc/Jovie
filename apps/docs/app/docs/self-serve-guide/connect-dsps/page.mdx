# Connect DSPs

Connecting your digital streaming platforms (DSPs) allows Jovie to
automatically import your catalog and generate smart links for your releases.

## Supported platforms

- Spotify
- Apple Music
- YouTube Music
- Amazon Music
- Tidal
- Deezer
- SoundCloud

## Steps

1. **Go to Integrations** -- From your dashboard, navigate to Settings then Integrations.
2. **Select a platform** -- Click "Connect" next to the platform you want to link.
3. **Authorize** -- Follow the OAuth flow to grant Jovie read access to your catalog.
4. **Import** -- Jovie will automatically pull in your releases. This may take a few minutes.

## Troubleshooting

- **Authorization failed** -- Make sure you are logging in with the correct account
  (your artist/creator account, not a personal listener account).
- **Missing releases** -- Some platforms have a delay before new releases appear
  in their API. Try again in 24 hours.
