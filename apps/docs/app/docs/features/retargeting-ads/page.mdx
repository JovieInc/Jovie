# Retargeting Ads

Turn anonymous profile visitors into ad audiences. Jovie forwards pixel events to Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google, and TikTok so you can retarget people who've already shown interest in your music.

## How retargeting works with Jovie

1. **Visitor lands on your Jovie page** -- They browse your profile, click a smart link, or subscribe.
2. **Pixel fires** -- Jovie sends the event to your connected ad platforms (Facebook, Google, TikTok).
3. **Audience builds** -- Your ad platform collects these visitors into a retargeting audience.
4. **You run ads** -- Target ads specifically to people who've already interacted with your music.

## Why this matters

Most link-in-bio tools send fans to a page and that's the end of the story. With Jovie's pixel integration, every visitor becomes a potential ad audience member. This means:

- **Lower ad costs** -- Retargeting warm audiences converts better than cold targeting.
- **Smarter spending** -- Only advertise to people who've already shown interest.
- **Full funnel** -- Drive fans from awareness (streaming) to action (tickets, merch, subscribe).

## Supported platforms

- **Meta (Facebook + Instagram)** -- Facebook Pixel with Conversions API (CAPI) support for reliable server-side tracking.
- **Google** -- Google Analytics integration for audience building and Google Ads retargeting.
- **TikTok** -- TikTok Pixel for retargeting on the platform where many fans discover music.

## Key capabilities

- **Server-side tracking** -- Facebook CAPI ensures events fire even when client-side tracking is blocked.
- **Consent-aware** -- Pixel events respect visitor consent preferences.
- **UTM tracking** -- Incoming UTM parameters are captured and forwarded for campaign attribution.
- **Event types** -- Page views, link clicks, form submissions, and purchases are all tracked.

## Plan availability

Retargeting ads are available on Pro and Growth plans.

## Getting started

1. Set up your ad pixels in **Settings > Ad Pixels**. See [Ad Pixel Tracking](/docs/features/analytics/ad-pixels).
2. Configure retargeting settings in **Settings > Retargeting Ads**.
3. Build audiences in your ad platform using the pixel data.
