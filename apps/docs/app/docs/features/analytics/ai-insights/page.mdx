# AI-Powered Insights

Jovie's AI analyzes your analytics data and generates actionable insights — so you can focus on making music instead of staring at dashboards.

## How it works

Jovie runs AI analysis on your profile data, click events, audience demographics, and engagement patterns. The result is a feed of prioritized insights, each with a clear recommendation.

## Insight categories

- **Engagement** -- Which releases, links, or pages are driving the most interaction. What's working and what isn't.
- **Geographic** -- Where your fans are growing. Emerging cities and countries. Touring opportunities based on audience concentration.
- **Device** -- How your fans access your content. Mobile vs desktop trends. Platform preferences.
- **Audience quality** -- The health of your fan base. Engagement depth, return visit rates, and subscriber conversion.
- **Momentum** -- Trends over time. Are you growing, plateauing, or declining in key metrics?

## Priority ranking

Each insight is ranked by priority:

- **High** -- Act on this now. Significant opportunity or concern.
- **Medium** -- Worth attention. Plan to address soon.
- **Low** -- Good to know. No immediate action needed.

You can dismiss insights you've reviewed or acted on. Dismissed insights won't resurface.

## Plan availability

AI-powered insights are available on Pro and Growth plans.

## Getting started

Visit your **Insights dashboard** at `/dashboard/insights`. Insights are generated automatically based on your data — no setup required.
