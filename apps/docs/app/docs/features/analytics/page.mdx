# Analytics & Insights

Track your performance across every part of Jovie with detailed analytics. Understand who your fans are, where they come from, and what they do — so you can make better decisions about your music career.

## Dashboard overview

Your analytics dashboard at `/dashboard/analytics` gives you a high-level view of:

- **Profile views** -- How many people visited your Jovie page.
- **Link clicks** -- Which smart links are getting the most engagement, broken down by platform.
- **Top referrers** -- Where your traffic is coming from (Instagram, Twitter, Google, direct, etc.).
- **Audience growth** -- New subscribers and visitors over time.

## Device & location data

Understand your audience at a granular level:

- **Device type** -- Mobile vs desktop breakdown, plus iOS vs Android split.
- **Operating system** -- Which OS your fans use.
- **Browser** -- Chrome, Safari, Firefox, etc.
- **Geographic data** -- Country and city-level breakdown of where your fans are.

## Data retention

How long Jovie stores your analytics data depends on your plan:

| Plan | Retention |
|---|---|
| Free | 30 days |
| Pro | 90 days |
| Growth | 1 year |

## Advanced analytics (Pro+)

- **Geographic insights** -- Deep dive into which cities and countries drive the most engagement. Identify emerging markets and touring opportunities.
- **Filter your own visits** -- Exclude your own traffic from analytics so you see real fan data only.
- **AI-powered insights** -- Jovie's AI analyzes your data and generates actionable insights about engagement patterns, geographic opportunities, audience quality, and momentum trends. [Learn more](/docs/features/analytics/ai-insights).

## Ad pixel tracking (Pro+)

Install tracking pixels to retarget your Jovie visitors across ad platforms. [Learn more](/docs/features/analytics/ad-pixels).

- **Facebook pixel** -- Retarget visitors on Facebook and Instagram.
- **Google Analytics** -- Track Jovie traffic in your Google Analytics dashboard.
- **TikTok pixel** -- Retarget visitors on TikTok.

## Export

Export your analytics data as CSV for further analysis, reporting, or sharing with your team.
