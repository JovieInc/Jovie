# Analytics

Track your performance across every part of the Jovie platform with detailed
analytics.

## Dashboard overview

Your analytics dashboard gives you a high-level view of profile visits, link
clicks, audience growth, and revenue from tips.

## Key metrics

- **Profile views** -- How many people visited your Jovie page.
- **Link clicks** -- Which smart links are getting the most engagement.
- **Audience growth** -- New followers over time.
- **Top referrers** -- Where your traffic is coming from.
- **Tip revenue** -- Total tips received and average tip amount.

## Export

Export your analytics data as CSV for further analysis or reporting.
