# Releases & Smart Links

Manage your music releases and share them with smart links that route every fan to their preferred streaming platform — no friction, no wasted clicks.

For the launch path, releases are not optional polish. Your Spotify-first
onboarding only completes once at least one visible release is available on the
public profile.

## Smart links

Every release on Jovie gets a smart link: a single URL that detects the listener's device, preferred platform, and location, then routes them to the right place.

- **One link, every platform** -- Share a single URL that works for Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, Amazon Music, Tidal, Deezer, SoundCloud, and more.
- **Deep links to native apps** -- Links open directly in the streaming app instead of a browser, reducing friction for the listener.
- **Auto DSP detection** -- Jovie's MusicFetch integration automatically finds your release across all major platforms and creates the links.
- **Short links** -- Compact URLs via `jov.ie/r/{slug}` for social media bios and posts.
- **Vanity URLs** -- Custom slugs for your releases (e.g., `jov.ie/{username}/my-album`).
- **Edit & customize** -- Reorder platforms, customize CTAs, and control the appearance of your smart link pages.

## Release management

- **Auto-sync from Spotify** -- Connect your Spotify and Jovie imports your entire catalog automatically.
- **Manual release creation** -- Add releases that aren't on streaming platforms yet, or create entries for non-music content.
- **Release pages** -- Each release gets a public page with album artwork, track listing, and listen buttons for every available DSP.
- **Track-level detail** -- View duration, ISRC codes, and per-track smart links.

## Pre-release features (Pro+)

- **Countdown pages** -- Create landing pages for upcoming releases with a countdown timer.
- **Pre-save campaigns** -- Let fans pre-save your release on Spotify and Apple Music before it drops. Jovie handles the OAuth flow and triggers the save on release day.
- **Release notifications** -- Email your subscribers when a new release drops. Configure preview and release-day notifications separately.

## "Use This Sound" pages

Share your tracks with content creators and influencers. Each release has a sounds page at `/{username}/{release}/sounds` with direct links to:

- **TikTok** -- "Use sound on TikTok"
- **Instagram Reels** -- "Use audio on Instagram"
- **YouTube Shorts** -- "Use sound on YouTube"

Video provider links are populated automatically by MusicFetch. If no video links exist, the page redirects to the main smart link.

## Getting started

1. [Connect your DSPs](/docs/self-serve-guide/connect-dsps) to auto-import your catalog.
2. Finish onboarding once your first visible release appears.
3. Confirm the release is visible on `jov.ie/{username}` and your smart-link pages.
4. Share your smart links in social bios, stories, emails, and anywhere fans find you.
