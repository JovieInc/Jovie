# Features

Jovie is a complete toolkit for music creators — smart links, artist profiles, audience growth, analytics, monetization, and AI assistance. Here's everything included.

- [Profile](/docs/features/profile) -- Your public artist page with bio, social links, tour dates, and a smart link-in-bio that adapts to every visitor.
- [Releases & Smart Links](/docs/features/releases) -- Manage your catalog and share smart links that route fans to the right streaming platform. Includes "Use This Sound" pages for influencer sharing.
- [Audience](/docs/features/audience) -- Build and understand your fan base with automatic visitor tracking, subscriber capture, and a full CRM.
- [Analytics](/docs/features/analytics) -- Track profile views, link clicks, geographic data, and get AI-powered insights about your audience.
- [AI Assistant](/docs/features/chat-ai) -- An AI career assistant that helps with profile optimization, release strategy, analytics interpretation, and growth recommendations.
- [Tips & Payments](/docs/features/tips) -- Accept tips from fans directly on your profile. Track earnings and payouts.
- [Retargeting Ads](/docs/features/retargeting-ads) -- Install Facebook, Google, and TikTok pixels to retarget your Jovie visitors across ad platforms.
