# Fan CRM

Go beyond basic audience tracking with Jovie's built-in contact management system. Organize your professional contacts, track relationships, and export your list for use in other tools.

## Contact management

- **Unlimited contacts** -- Pro and Growth plans remove the 100-contact limit on Free.
- **Contact roles** -- Tag contacts as manager, booking agent, distributor, publicist, or custom roles.
- **Contact channels** -- Store email, phone, and DM handles for each contact.
- **Territory assignment** -- Assign contacts to geographic territories for regional management.
- **Preferred contact method** -- Note whether someone prefers email, phone, or DM.

## Contact export

Export your full contact list as a CSV file. Use it in:

- Email tools (ConvertKit, etc.)
- External CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce, etc.)
- Spreadsheets for custom analysis

## Plan availability

| Capability | Free | Pro | Growth |
|---|---|---|---|
| Contact limit | 100 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Contact roles & channels | -- | Yes | Yes |
| Contact export (CSV) | -- | Yes | Yes |

## Getting started

1. Visit your **Contacts dashboard** at `/dashboard/contacts`.
2. Contacts are collected automatically from notification opt-ins.
3. Add roles, channels, and territories to organize your network.
4. Export anytime from the dashboard.
