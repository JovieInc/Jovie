# Audience & Growth

Build, understand, and grow your fan base with Jovie's audience tools. Every interaction with your profile — views, clicks, notification opt-ins — builds your audience automatically.

## Fan collection

Every visit to your Jovie page tracks engagement automatically:

- **Anonymous visitors** -- Device type, location (city and country), browser, OS, and referrer are tracked even for visitors who don't opt in.
- **Fan notifications** -- Fans who opt in provide their email and get notified automatically when you release new music or go on tour.
- **Intent tracking** -- Jovie scores each visitor's intent level (low, medium, high) based on their behavior — clicks, time on page, and interactions.
- **Engagement scoring** -- Each audience member gets an engagement score based on visit frequency, actions taken, and recency.

## Audience dashboard

View all your audience data at `/dashboard/audience`:

- **Visitor list** -- See who's visited your profile, when, and what they did.
- **Geographic data** -- Where your fans are located, by city and country.
- **Device breakdown** -- Mobile vs desktop, iOS vs Android.
- **Growth trends** -- How your audience is growing over time.

## Fan CRM (Pro+)

Go beyond basic audience tracking with full contact management. [Learn more](/docs/features/audience/crm).

- **Contact management** -- Organize contacts with roles (manager, booking agent, distributor), channels (email, phone, DM), and territories.
- **Unlimited contacts** -- Free plans are limited to 100 contacts. Pro and Growth plans have no limit.
- **Contact export** -- Export your contact list as CSV for use in email marketing tools, CRMs, or spreadsheets.

## Release notifications

Fans get notified when you drop new music. Configure per-release:

- **Preview notifications** -- Alert fans before a release drops.
- **Release day notifications** -- Notify fans on release day with a smart link.

## Getting started

Your audience builds automatically as soon as your profile is live. To accelerate growth:

1. Share your notification link (`jov.ie/{username}/notifications`) in your social bios.
2. Use your Jovie QR code at live shows.
3. Check your audience dashboard regularly to understand who your fans are and where they're coming from.
