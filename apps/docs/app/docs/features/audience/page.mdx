# Audience

Build and understand your fan base with Jovie's audience management tools.

## Fan collection

Every interaction with your Jovie page -- profile views, link clicks, pre-saves --
helps build your audience automatically. Fans can also opt in to follow you for
updates.

## Segmentation

Group your audience by engagement level, location, platform preference, and more.
Use segments to target your messaging and promotions to the right fans.

## Growth tracking

Monitor how your audience grows over time and identify which channels drive
the most new fans.
