# Verified Badge

The verified badge appears next to your artist name on your Jovie profile, signaling legitimacy and professionalism to fans, bookers, curators, and industry contacts.

## What it signals

- **Authenticity** -- Confirms this is the official profile for the artist.
- **Professionalism** -- Shows you're invested in your online presence.
- **Trust** -- Bookers, curators, and collaborators can trust they're reaching the right person.

## How to get verified

The verified badge is available on Pro and Growth plans. Once you upgrade:

1. Your profile automatically displays the verified badge.
2. The badge appears next to your display name on your public profile.
3. It's visible to every visitor — fans, industry contacts, and anyone who views your page.
