# Tour Dates

Display your upcoming shows directly on your Jovie profile, synced from Bandsintown. Fans see your tour schedule at `jov.ie/{username}/tour` with venue details and ticket links.

## How it works

1. **Connect Bandsintown** -- Enter your Bandsintown artist name in Settings > Touring.
2. **Automatic sync** -- Jovie pulls your tour dates from the Bandsintown API automatically.
3. **Fan experience** -- Visitors see your upcoming shows sorted by date, with venue name, city, and a direct link to buy tickets.

## Key capabilities

- **Automatic sync** -- No manual entry needed. Add dates in Bandsintown and they appear on your Jovie profile.
- **Geographic proximity** -- Tour dates include latitude and longitude data, enabling location-aware features.
- **Ticket links** -- Each show links directly to the ticket purchase page.
- **Timezone support** -- Shows display in the correct timezone for each venue.
- **Dedicated tour page** -- Your tour schedule lives at `/{username}/tour`, shareable as a standalone link.

## Plan availability

Tour dates are available on all plans, including Free.

## Getting started

1. Go to **Settings > Touring** in your dashboard.
2. Enter your Bandsintown artist name.
3. Your tour dates will sync automatically and appear on your profile.

See the [Connect Bandsintown guide](/docs/self-serve-guide/connect-bandsintown) for detailed setup instructions.
