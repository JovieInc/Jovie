# Profile

Your Jovie profile is the central hub for your artist presence online. It
serves as a landing page where fans can discover your music, learn about you,
and connect with you across platforms.

## Key capabilities

- **Custom handle** -- A short, memorable URL like `jovie.com/yourname`.
- **Bio and photos** -- Showcase who you are with a rich biography and gallery.
- **Social links** -- Connect all your social media accounts in one place.
- **Release showcase** -- Automatically display your latest releases with smart links.

## Customization

You can customize your profile colors, layout, and featured content from the
dashboard. Changes are reflected immediately on your public page.
