# Artist Profile

Your Jovie profile is the public page you ship during onboarding. Once your
Spotify-first setup is complete and a visible release is present, fans can land
on `jov.ie/{username}`, hear your music, and subscribe for updates immediately.

## Your public page

Every Jovie artist gets a public profile at `jov.ie/{username}`. This page includes:

- **Display name and avatar** -- Your artist identity, front and center.
- **Profile details** -- Your location, story, and creator context from the dashboard settings.
- **Social links** -- Connect all your platforms so fans can keep following you after the first visit.
- **Release showcase** -- Your latest music and listen surfaces, updated from your catalog.
- **Fan subscribe entrypoint** -- Fans can turn on notifications directly from the profile.
- **Tour dates** -- Upcoming shows synced from Bandsintown with venue, date, and ticket links. [Learn more](/docs/features/profile/tour-dates).

## Sub-pages

Your profile includes dedicated pages for specific interactions:

- **Listen** (`/{username}/listen`) -- Routes fans to your music on their preferred streaming platform.
- **Subscribe** (`/{username}/subscribe`) -- Fans opt in for email updates that feed your audience list.
- **Contact** (`/{username}/contact`) -- Professional contact form for booking inquiries, press, and business.
- **About** (`/{username}/about`) -- Extended biography and background.
- **Tour** (`/{username}/tour`) -- All upcoming tour dates with ticket links.
- **Tip** (`/{username}/tip`) -- Accept tips from fans (Pro+).
- **Use This Sound** (`/{username}/{release}/sounds`) -- Let influencers use your tracks in TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.

## Customization

- **Custom handle** -- A short, memorable URL like `jov.ie/yourname`. 3-30 characters, alphanumeric with hyphens and underscores.
- **Theme and colors** -- Customize your profile's appearance from the dashboard.
- **Remove Jovie branding** (Max) -- Remove the "Powered by Jovie" footer for a fully white-labeled experience.
- **Verified badge** (Pro+) -- A verification badge that signals legitimacy to fans, bookers, and curators. [Learn more](/docs/features/profile/verified-badge).
