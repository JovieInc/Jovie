# Tips

Accept tips and direct financial support from your fans through your Jovie
profile.

## Setup

Connect your payment account through the dashboard to start accepting tips.
Jovie supports Stripe for secure payment processing.

## Fan experience

Fans can leave a tip from your profile page with a single click. They can
choose from preset amounts or enter a custom amount, and optionally leave a
message.

## Payouts

Tips are deposited to your connected bank account on a regular schedule. You
can track all incoming tips and messages from your dashboard.
