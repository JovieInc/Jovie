# Tips & Payments

Accept tips and direct financial support from your fans through your Jovie profile. Turn live shows, social media moments, and genuine fan appreciation into revenue.

## How it works

1. **Connect your payment account** -- Set up Venmo through your dashboard settings to start accepting tips.
2. **Share your tip link** -- Your profile includes a dedicated tip page at `jov.ie/{username}/tip`. Share it at shows, in social bios, or anywhere fans can find you.
3. **Fans tip instantly** -- Visitors choose from preset amounts or enter a custom amount, with an optional message.
4. **Track everything** -- Monitor all incoming tips, messages, and payout history from your earnings dashboard.

## Key capabilities

- **One-tap tipping** -- Fans can tip from your profile with minimal friction. No account required.
- **Custom and preset amounts** -- Offer suggested tip amounts or let fans choose their own.
- **Fan messages** -- Fans can leave a note with their tip. See all messages in your dashboard.
- **Earnings dashboard** -- Track total tips received, individual transactions, and payout status at a glance.
- **QR code friendly** -- Perfect for live shows. Fans scan your QR code and the fastest tip flow opens immediately.

## Getting started

1. Go to **Settings > Billing** and upgrade to Pro if needed.
2. Connect your Venmo account in **Settings**.
3. Share your tip link: `jov.ie/{username}/tip`.
