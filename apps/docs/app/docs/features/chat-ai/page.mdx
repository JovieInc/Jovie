# AI Assistant

Jovie's AI assistant helps you make smarter decisions about your music career. Ask questions about your analytics, get release strategy advice, optimize your profile, and plan your next move — all from a conversational interface.

## What Jovie AI can do

- **Profile optimization** -- Get suggestions to improve your bio, social links, and profile presentation based on what's working for similar artists.
- **Release strategy** -- Plan release timing, pre-save campaigns, and promotion strategies.
- **Analytics interpretation** -- Ask about your data in plain language. "Where are my fans located?" or "Which release is performing best?"
- **Profile editing** -- Ask the AI to update your bio, add social links, or create releases directly through the conversation.
- **Growth recommendations** -- Actionable suggestions based on your audience data, engagement patterns, and market trends.

## How it works

1. Open the AI assistant from your dashboard sidebar.
2. Start a conversation — ask anything about your music career or Jovie features.
3. The AI references your profile data, analytics, and audience to give personalized advice.
4. When you approve a suggestion, the AI can make changes to your profile directly.

All conversations are saved so you can pick up where you left off.
