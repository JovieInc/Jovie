# Chat and AI

Communicate with your fans using Jovie's built-in chat, powered by AI to help
you stay responsive even when you are busy.

## Direct messaging

Fans can message you directly through your Jovie profile. All conversations
are organized in a single inbox on your dashboard.

## AI assistance

Jovie's AI can draft replies, answer common questions about your music and
upcoming shows, and help you maintain a consistent voice across conversations.
You always have the final say before any message is sent.

## Broadcast messages

Send announcements to your entire audience or specific segments. Broadcast
messages are great for release announcements, tour dates, and exclusive content.
