# Artist Profiles Landing Page Features

| Feature | User Benefit | Proving UI Surface | One-Line Copy Candidate | Placement |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Adaptive profile | One link can match the moment instead of forcing fans through a static page. | Pinned phone mode switcher | One profile that changes job as the moment changes. | section |
| Speed | Fewer drop-offs when attention is thin. | Hero render, performance framing in spec wall | Fast enough to keep the tap alive. | spec tile |
| Conversion-focused design | Fans know what to do next without extra decisions. | Hero, outcomes cards, opinionated section | Built to convert, not decorate. | section |
| Intelligent routing | Fans reach the right service faster. | Release day mode, release pages, routing tile | Send each fan where they are most likely to listen. | spec tile |
| Deep-link modes | One profile can handle listen, tour, support, and subscribe states. | Mode switcher | Listen, tour, support, and subscribe all live behind one profile. | section |

| Shows / tickets | Touring fans see nearby show intent faster. | Touring mode, tour tile | Nearby dates come first. | card |

| Fan capture | Traffic can become an owned audience. | Capture Every Fan section, fan capture tile | Capture the fan, not just the click. | section |
| Notifications | Fans can stay in the loop after they opt in once. | Notifications state, capture section, notifications tile | Fans opt in once and stay close. | card |

## Nice To Mention Later

- Verified badge
- Contact and about depth
- Social auto-suggestions from connected platforms
- Advanced analytics
- AI
- Export / CRM detail
- Remove-branding or white-label detail

## Do Not Mention On This Page

- Theme builders
- Open-ended customization
- Generic creator websites
- Fake conversion stats
- AI assistant positioning
- Broad "all creators" language
- White-label positioning
- Anything that implies Jovie is a website builder

## Placement Defaults

- Adaptive profile, Capture Every Fan, and Opinionated by Design deserve full sections.
- Release, touring, support, notifications, and QR behavior belong in the mode switcher or outcomes cards.
- Speed, routing, QR, and restrained analytics belong in the spec wall.

## Recommended Spec Wall Density

- Use a medium-dense spec wall with 8 to 10 compact tiles.
- Keep each tile to a label and a maximum of two short lines.
- The wall should read like product truth, not a feature dump.
- Analytics should stay secondary and compact, framed only as views, clicks, and referrers.
