## Vision

### 10x Check

Instead of just asking for a name, build the fan identity layer — the foundation that makes every future fan interaction personal. The moment after email capture is a golden window: the fan just took an action, they're engaged, they're on the artist's page. Use that moment to start a relationship, not just collect a data point.

### Platonic Ideal

The fan subscribes. The success state transforms into a warm, delightful moment. "What should we call you?" — a single name field, skip always visible. If they enter their name, confirmation shifts to "Thanks, Sarah!" From that moment on, every notification says "Hey Sarah" instead of "Hey there." The creator's dashboard shows "Sarah" instead of "j.doe@gmail.com." The entire interaction takes 3 seconds and feels like a conversation, not data extraction.
