## How This Works

- People on this list are people Tim personally respects and wants to talk to
- They don't need to be people we have existing relationships with
- The list expands over time as Tim adds names
- Each person gets a "status" tracking our progress toward getting them
- Eventually this becomes our podcast booking pipeline

## Status Legend

- 🔴 Not contacted — on the list, no outreach yet
- 🟡 Warm intro — someone can make an introduction
- 🟢 In conversation — actively pursuing
- ✅ Booked — confirmed for show/podcast
- 🎬 Appeared — already appeared in content
