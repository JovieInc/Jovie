# BUBBLEGUM FACTORY — CONTENT BIBLE

The show bible for the Bubblegum Factory content universe.

## The Pitch

**Entourage + The Office + Silicon Valley + Keeping Up With The Kardashians** for the AI creator era.

The insight: people don't watch shows about software. They watch shows about **people**. Right now, Jovie marketing is one character (founder builds AI startup). The Bubblegum Factory creates an entire universe.

## The Characters

### Tim White — Founder
- Real person, playing a version of himself
- Smart, honest, building something real
- Sometimes wrong, always genuine
- Founder energy: calm, thoughtful, slightly obsessive
- **Content purpose**: Builds trust, makes Jovie feel human

### Jovie — Virtual Co-Host
- AI character with opinions, taste, and personality
- Sharp, opinionated, artistically fluent
- Sometimes right about artists, sometimes wrong — audiences track her record
- Represents the Jovie AI platform as a character
- **Content purpose**: Makes AI relatable, creates debate, builds IP

### Artists (Rotating Cast)
- Real musicians helped by Jovie
- Their actual careers become storylines
- Audiences watch real outcomes from the show
- **Content purpose**: Proof of Jovie's value, emotional investment

## Show Formats (Launch Order)

### 1. Short-Form Podcast Clips
- **Duration**: 30-90 seconds
- **Platform**: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
- **Format**: Tim responding to unseen interviewer
- **Frequency**: 3-5x per week to start
- **Purpose**: Build audience, establish set, build brands

### 2. Founder Confessionals
- **Duration**: 30-90 seconds
- **Platform**: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
- **Format**: Tim speaking intimately/tightly framed about building
- **Frequency**: 1-2x per week
- **Purpose**: Deepen Tim's character, emotional connection

### 3. Full Podcast Episodes
- **Duration**: 5-30 minutes
- **Platform**: YouTube + podcast platforms
- **Format**: Tim + Jovie discussing artist topics
- **Frequency**: 1-2x per week
- **Purpose**: Long-form depth, character development

### 4. Documentary Content
- **Duration**: 2-10 minutes
- **Platform**: YouTube
- **Format**: Behind-the-scenes, artist profiles, Factory tour
- **Frequency**: 1x per week
- **Purpose**: Expand the world, build mythology

### 5. Full TV Episodes (Future)
- **Duration**: 15-45 minutes
- **Platform**: YouTube / streaming
- **Format**: Produced episode with story arc
- **Frequency**: Weekly (seasonal)
- **Purpose**: Premium content, monetization, platform deals

## Brand Voice

### Tim's Voice
- Honest, direct, founder-to-founder energy
- Not a hype man. Not a guru.
- Says what founders think but don't say
- Occasionally vulnerable, always genuine

### Jovie's Voice
- Sharp, quick, aesthetically opinionated
- Has taste and articulates why
- Self-confident but not arrogant
- Will say "I was wrong" — audiences love Accountability

### The Factory's Voice
- Mysterious, confident, minimal
- "Manufacturing Pop Since 2014"
- Never explains too much
- Lets the world speak for itself

- **Intro/outro**: Signature Bubblegum Factory sound (TBD, should be ownable)

## Caption/Post-Production Standards

### Captions
- Added in post (CapCut / Premiere / Figma)
- Clean, readable, branded
- Tim's name + topic as text overlay
- CTA: follow, comment, subscribe

### Text on Screen
- NEVER generated in AI — always composited
- Use consistent fonts and placement
- Bubblegum Factory watermark in corner (subtle)

### Thumbnail Standard
- Vertical crop of podcast frame
- Tim visible + microphone + hot pink accent
- Bold text overlay (added in post)
- Consistent branded look

## Success Metrics

### Phase 1 (Month 1-2): Foundation
- **Followers**: Build to 10K combined across platforms
- **Content**: 40+ clips shipped, room is locked and consistent
- **Audience**: Are people following? Are they asking "Is this a real place?"

### Phase 2 (Month 3-4): Growth
- **Followers**: 50K+ combined
- **Engagement**: Comments asking about the Factory, requesting merch
- **Podcast**: Full episodes getting 1K+ views

### Phase 3 (Month 5+): IP + Monetization
- **Merch**: First Factory shirts sellable
- **Partnerships**: Real artists featured on the show
- **Platform**: Shopping format to streamers/YouTube Originals
