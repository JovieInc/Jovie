# The Bubblegum Factory

**Manufacturing Pop Since 2014**

The Bubblegum Factory is a fictional/virtual entertainment studio in Los Angeles. It's the central location of the Bubblegum Factory cinematic universe — a futuristic pop-music manufacturing facility where music gets made, artists get built, and pop culture gets engineered.

The Factory is real, virtual, fictional, and true all at once.

## What It Is

1. **A virtual podcast studio** — all-white futuristic set where Tim White and Jovie discuss artist problems, music industry truths, and startup life
2. **A content factory** — the production hub for short-form video content across TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels
3. **An entertainment IP** — the foundation for a future episodic show blending Entourage + The Office + Silicon Valley for the AI creator era
4. **A brand universe** — merchandise, lore, and a world that feels real enough that people ask "is that a real place?"
5. **A future real location** — the fictional Factory will eventually become a physical place

## The World

### Premise
Tim White runs The Bubblegum Factory — a futuristic pop-music manufacturing facility in Los Angeles. Inside, he builds Jovie (an AI music platform) alongside Jovie (a virtual character), helps real artists build careers, and manufactures pop culture.

### Visual Identity
- **All-white, glossy, circular, minimalist**
- **No hard corners** — rounded walls, curved furniture, seamless panels
- **Only accent color: vivid bubblegum hot pink (#FF69B4)**
- **Materials:** matte white acoustic fabric, glossy white polymer, brushed aluminum, translucent plexiglass
- **Feels like:** luxury recording studio + futuristic laboratory + architectural magazine interior

### Slogan
> Manufacturing Pop Since 2014

### 1. Podcast Room ⭐ (FIRST BUILD)

### 2. Lobby (Homepage Hero)
- Large circular all-white futuristic space
- Sunken circular conversation pit with rounded white couches
- Circular white reception desk
- Massive white blast doors with hot pink emblem
- Public-facing espresso bar
- Dishes of bubblegum-pink gum on tables

### 3. Blast Doors
- Massive elegant futuristic white doors
- Soft white LED seams, rounded panel geometry

### 4. Hallway (Millennium Falcon-style)
- All-white octagonal/circular corridor
- Seamless curved wall panels, soft glowing LED seams
- Removable brushed-metal floor grates

### 5. Control Room

- Professional equipment: monitors, patch bays, screens, faders

### 6. Live Room
- Seamless curved white acoustic walls

- White grand piano, drum kit, vocal mic stands, guitar amps
