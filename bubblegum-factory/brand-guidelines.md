# Brand Guidelines — The Bubblegum Factory

## Colors

| Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|------|-----|-----|-------|
| Factory White | #F8F8F8 | 248, 248, 248 | Primary surfaces |
| Acoustic White | #EBEBEB | 235, 235, 235 | Wall panels |
| Gloss White | #FFFFFF | 255, 255, 255 | Table, counters |
| Shadow Gray | #D0D0D0 | 208, 208, 208 | Depth variation |
| **Bubblegum Pink** | **#FF69B4** | **255, 105, 180** | **Logos, accents, merch ONLY** |
| Black Metal | #1A1A1A | 26, 26, 26 | Microphones, hardware |

## Slogan
> Manufacturing Pop Since 2014

## Taglines
- "Manufacturing Culture Since 2014"
- "The Future of Pop Music Is Manufactured Here"
- "Manufactured in Hollywood Since 2014"
- "Manufacturing the Future of Pop Since 2014"

## Logo Treatment
- Hot pink block stencil / stamped factory mark
- Thick block letters, slightly distressed edges
- Like a flight case stamp or warehouse customs mark
- NEVER thin neon tubing
- ETCHED variant: machined into table surface with soft glow

## Motif
```
Manufactured at The Bubblegum Factory
Los Angeles, California
```

## Typography
- Primary: Clean sans-serif (Helvetica, Inter, or similar)
- Logo: Custom block stencil style
- Captions: Bold sans-serif, high contrast on video

## Visual Rules
1. Everything is white except one accent: hot pink
2. No hard corners — all curves, all circles
3. No readable text in AI-generated images
4. No dark lighting — always bright, expensive, soft
5. No clutter — everything intentional

## Set Design Rules
1. Circular rooms preferred
2. Acoustic panels in horizontal rows
3. Soft LED seams between panels
4. Brushed aluminum hardware
5. Professional black microphones as only dark objects
