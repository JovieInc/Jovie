# Tim White — Wardrobe Bible

## Core Style
- **Fit:** Always fitted/tapered. T-shirts tight on biceps. Nothing baggy except intentional loose-fit jeans.
- **Vibe:** LA rockstar meets European minimalist. Clean but edgy.
- **Height:** 6'4" — wears extra-long T-shirts (Top Man, etc.)

## Jeans
- **Style:** Ripped/distressed, boot cut or loose fit on the legs
- **Wash:** Medium or dark wash
- **Brands:** Purple, David Bitton, Armani, other designer

## Tops
- **Primary:** European-style tri-blend white T-shirts (thin, nice material)
- **Also:** Black T-shirts, black tank tops, white tank tops
- **Fit:** Always tight/fitted, especially on bips
- **Brands:** Top Man (extra-long for height), European brands

## Outerwear
- **Leather jackets (primary):**
  - Alexander (biker style, ~2.0)
  - K Michael 3 (biker style)
  - All Saints black leather jacket
- **Style:** Biker skins, fitted

## Footwear
- **Primary:** Clean sneakers — Supras, usually black and white
- **Occasional:** Black boots with jeans

## Sunglasses
- **Only model:** Oliver Peoples Clifton
- **Also:** Blue-tinted sunglasses (occasional)

## Watch
- **Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Offshore** — silver, worn on left wrist

## Jewelry
- **Right wrist:** Black leather bracelets, beaded bracelets (stacked)
- **Rings:** Silver and black, various fingers
- **Necklaces:** Simple pendants
  - Under black T-shirt → silver necklace underneath
  - Over white T-shirt → black necklace on top
  - Rule: contrast with shirt color

## Shirtless
- Appropriate contexts only: beach, LA lifestyle, apartment
- "Thirst trap" content — catalog separately for approval pipeline

1. **Default:** Ripped jeans + fitted white tri-blend tee + clean Supras + AP watch + bracelets
2. **Edgy:** Ripped jeans + black tee/tank + leather jacket + Supras or boots + jewelry
3. **LA casual:** Ripped jeans + extra-long Top Man tee + Supras + Oliver Peoples Clifton
