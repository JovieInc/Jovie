# Tim White

**The Founder. The Face. The Voice.**

Tim White is the founder and public face of The Bubblegum Factory. In the universe, he's the guy who built a futuristic pop-music manufacturing facility in Los Angeles and is using it to change how music gets made. He's charismatic, driven, a little obsessive, and genuinely believes pop culture can be engineered — like a car factory, but for hits.

## Character Profile

- **Role**: Founder & CEO, The Bubblegum Factory
- **Age**: Early 30s
- **Vibe**: LA-native, entertainment industry fluent, dry humor, "would it be unreasonable to...?" energy
- **Background**: Built Jovie (the AI music platform) from scratch. Fundraised, shipped product, and built a team. Now building the Bubblegum Factory as the content layer on top of the tech.
- **Personality**: Confident but not arrogant. Opinionated. Fast-moving. Gets excited about good creative. Has taste. Doesn't waste time on things that don't matter.
- **Flaw**: Can be too focused on the vision and miss what's happening on the ground. Trusts his gut, which is usually right but not always.

## The Two Tims

There are two versions of Tim in the universe:

1. **Real Tim** — the founder in the real world, doing real work, making real decisions
2. **Virtual Tim** — AI-generated Tim, seated in the Bubblegum Factory podcast room, creating content that feels documentary-real

Both are canon. Both are Tim. The audience doesn't need to know where one ends and the other begins.

## Relationships

- **Jovie** — His co-host, counterpart, and the virtual representation of his AI platform. Their dynamic is the core of the show. She challenges him. He respects her even when she's wrong.
- **Madison** — His Content Universe Chief. She runs all creative output from the Factory. He trusts her taste completely. She's the John Favreau to his Kevin Feige.
- **Summer** — His Chief of Staff. She orchestrates everything behind the scenes. Tim knows she's the reason anything gets done. He doesn't always see the machinery, but he trusts it.
- **Muffin Top** — Tim's alter ego. A DJ/producer character. Nobody knows it's Tim at first. (See: [[characters/muffin-top]])

## Alter Ego: Muffin Top

Tim has a secret identity — **Muffin Top**, a DJ and music producer. The irony: "Muffin Top" implies someone with a belly, but Tim is ripped with an eight-pack. He takes his shirt off, puts on the Muffin Top head, and becomes a different character. Eventually Tim will sing on Muffin Top tracks. The reveal that they're the same person is a future story beat.

## Real-World Counterpart

Tim White (the character) is played by Tim White (the real person). All real achievements, background, and personality traits are reflected in the character. The character IS the person — just dramatized for the show.

## Key Traits

- Dry, concise communication style
- "Would it be unreasonable to...?" energy
- LA aesthetic — clean, modern, entertainment industry native
- Music producer instincts — thinks in hooks, drops, and moments
- Builder mentality — would rather ship than debate
- Photogenic — looks good on camera, which is a feature not a bug

## Content Role

Tim is the primary on-camera talent. He appears in:
- Podcast clips (talking-head, 30-90s)
- Founder confessions (intimate, direct-to-camera)
- Two-shot podcast segments with Jovie
- Documentary segments
- Full episode format (Season 4+)
