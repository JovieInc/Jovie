# Muffin Top

**The Alter Ego. The DJ. The Ironic Eight-Pack.**

Muffin Top is a DJ and music producer character in the Bubblegum Factory universe. He's a shirtless guy with an eight-pack who wears a "Muffin Top" head — a character head that implies someone with belly fat, creating an ironic contrast with his ripped physique.
