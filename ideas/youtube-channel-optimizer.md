# YouTube Channel Optimizer

## The Pitch
Email YouTube channel owners and tell them you can increase their views by X%. $30 paid trial, money-back guarantee if it doesn't meet the metric.

## How It Works
1. Scrape YouTube channels where we can make a measurable difference
2. Edit video titles, thumbnails, descriptions for optimization
3. Measure and learn — get better with every channel
4. Closed-loop user feedback
5. No app needed — just email delivery of reports

## Business Model
- **Landing page**: Static, Jovie subdomain, same design system
- **Agent**: Runs on hyperagent.com or local machine
- **Delivery**: Email report
- **Payment**: Stripe payment link via email
- **No app**: Entire business is landing page → agent → email → Stripe

## Metrics (Kill/Promote)
- **Target**: $500 MRR within 14 days or kill
- **Conversion**: >2% landing page → paid trial
- **Retention**: >60% trial → repeat/referral
