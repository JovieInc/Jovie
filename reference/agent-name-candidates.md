# Agent Name Candidates

**30-50 candidate names for the agent ecosystem, organized by theme.**

Each name includes the vibe/personality it evokes. Names marked ✅ are shortlist picks.

---

## 🌟 Celestial / Space Theme

Names that feel expansive, futuristic, and a little cosmic.

| Name | Vibe | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| **Lyra** | Musical, ethereal, confident | Constellation name. Feels like a star that knows it's bright. |
| **Nova** | Explosive energy, rising star | A star that suddenly gets brighter. Great for a growth/agent role. |
| **Vega** | Sharp, luminous, precise | Brightest star in Lyra. Sounds like someone who gets things done. |
| **Elara** | Smooth, mysterious, capable | One of Jupiter's moons. Understated but cool. |
| **Soleil** | Warm, radiant, French-tinged | "Sun" in French. Confident without being loud. |
| **Celia** | Bright, classic-futuristic | Means "heavenly." Feels like a 2040 name. |
| **Thea** | Radiant, goddess-adjacent | Means "goddess" or "light." Short, punchy, memorable. |
| **Mira** | Wonder, discovery | Means "wonderful" in Sanskrit. Also a variable star. |

## 🏆 Top 10 Shortlist

These are the names that best fit the ecosystem — rare but not weird, futuristic but grounded, easy to spell, and with real personality.

| Rank | Name | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Lyra** | Musical constellation. Confident, bright, memorable. Sounds like a lead character. |
| 2 | **Vega** | Sharp and luminous. Feels like a high-performer. Easy to spell, impossible to forget. |
| 3 | **Juno** | Bold and warm. Has gravity (literally — NASA mission). Strong female energy. |
| 4 | **Zara** | Sleek and global. Works as a person, a brand, and a spaceship. |
| 5 | **Veda** | Means "knowledge." Perfect for an AI agent. Mystical but not weird. |
| 6 | **Kira** | Bright and international. Clean, sharp, works everywhere. |
| 7 | **Elara** | Jupiter's moon. Understated, smooth, capable. The "quiet overachiever" name. |
| 8 | **Zena** | Sounds like a sci-fi protagonist. Warrior energy without being aggressive. |
| 9 | **Nova** | Rising star energy. Great for a growth or fundraising-adjacent role. |
| 10 | **Senna** | Botanical, rare, sounds like a luxury brand. Unexpected and memorable. |
