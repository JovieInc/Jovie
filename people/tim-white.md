# Tim White

**Founder. Creator. The real person behind the Bubblegum Factory.**

Tim White is the founder of Jovie (AI music platform) and the creator of the Bubblegum Factory universe. He's an LA-native entrepreneur and content creator who builds things fast and ships faster.

- **GitHub**: itstimwhite
