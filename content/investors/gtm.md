# Go-to-Market

Jovie does not depend on paid acquisition.

The GTM thesis is simple: get the release infrastructure wedge into the hands of artists who are already trying to ship consistently, then let the product create its own distribution.

## Why this works

The wedge is the music-first link in bio plus release infrastructure. That matters because it sits at the audience front door and inside the release loop. Once an artist is using Jovie for real releases, fans and peer artists see it every time the artist shares music.

## Primary channels

### Founder distribution

Initial distribution comes from Tim White's existing audience and music-industry network:

- 190K Instagram followers
- 500+ live performances
- deep credibility in the electronic music community

This is the fastest way to get the wedge into the hands of artists with real release activity.

### Product virality

Every Jovie profile and release page advertises Jovie. The long-term loop is:

artist imports catalog -> shares releases through Jovie -> fans and peer artists see the profile and smart links -> more artists sign up

### Pre-qualified outbound

Jovie targets artists already paying for fragmented tooling:

- bio-link tools
- smart-link tools
- marketing plugins

This is not broad cold prospecting. It is a wedge into artists who already feel the pain around each release and already spend money trying to solve it.

### Content and founder narrative

The founder has a strong right to speak on:

- release velocity
- release more music
- growth systems for musicians
- the difference between creation and coordination

That content compounds acquisition without requiring paid UA.

## What we are not doing

- No paid UA as the primary growth lever
- No agency-heavy acquisition motion
- No dependence on ad arbitrage

Paid retargeting is a downstream product feature for artists, not the company's core GTM.

## What we need to prove next

- Onboarding converts cold artists, not just founder traffic
- Artists who adopt Jovie ship more releases with less friction
- Release-loop retention is strong enough to compound
- Pre-qualified outbound can produce the first 100 paying artists
