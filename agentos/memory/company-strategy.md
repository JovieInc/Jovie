# Company Strategy

## Primary KPI

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|-----|-----------|--------|
| Revenue-bearing active artists | Artists who have connected at least one platform AND generated ≥$1 in earnings through Jovie | Maximize month-over-month growth |

**All agent decisions must route back to this KPI.** Features that don't increase revenue-bearing active artists or directly unblock the path to them are deprioritized.

## Business Model

| Tier | Price | Key limit |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Free | $0 | 50-fan notification cap |
| Pro | $39/mo | Full notifications, advanced analytics |
| Max | $149/mo | Everything Pro + priority support + extended history |

Reverse trial: 14-day Pro access on signup, downgrades to Free. Conversion window is the key activation metric.

## Core Thesis

Artists are owed their data. Jovie aggregates cross-platform royalty, streaming, and fan data that labels and DSPs currently fragment or withhold. The product is the command center artists never had.

## Competitive Moat

| Moat | Status |
|------|--------|
| Cross-DSP data aggregation | Live (Spotify, Apple Music, more in roadmap) |
| Agent-first architecture | In progress (AgentOS Phases 1–7) |
| Founder-as-featured-creator | Tim White Spotify ID `4u` as trust anchor |

Do not compete on bio-link or link-in-bio positioning. Jovie is a command center, not a webpage builder.

## Current Phase

Pre-PMF. Iteration speed is critical. Merge freeze policy applies only for mobile release cuts (see CLAUDE.md). Ship fast, instrument everything, validate KPI impact.

## Fundraising Context

Narrative: artist data sovereignty + agent-first automation. Investors should see a credible path from "command center for 1 artist" to "command center for 10,000 artists" without linear headcount growth.

Key milestone before raise: demonstrable agent-driven KPI improvement (one dept agent → measurable activation or revenue increase).
