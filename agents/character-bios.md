## Framework

Each agent/character gets:
- **Birthday** (informs age, zodiac, generational identity)
- **Age** (informs language style, cultural references, music taste)
- **Hometown** (informs accent, background, perspective)
- **Favorite Music** (informed by age + hometown)
- **When they came to LA** (backstory moment)
- **When they joined Bubblegum Factory** (origin story)
- **Personality traits** (informed by all of the above)
