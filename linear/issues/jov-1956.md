### Action

The primary failures to verify first are `text-white/50` and `text-white/54` in `ArtistProfileSpecWall.tsx` — these are the most likely to fail the 4.5:1 threshold. The `text-white/62` tokens in WaitlistOutcomeView and ArtistProfileMonetizationSection are borderline and depend on exact background color.

Recommend running axe-core against `/artist-profiles` and `/waitlist` pages in CI to confirm computed contrast ratios. These tokens would need to increase opacity or shift hue (e.g., to `text-white/70` or higher) to pass WCAG AA for body-size text.
