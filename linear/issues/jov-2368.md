Belt-and-suspenders: also check `process.env.NODE_ENV === 'development'`.

Per autoplan eng review (F2): `NODE_ENV === 'production'` check inside a page component is not enough on its own — preview deploys also run with `NODE_ENV=production`. The env flag is the canonical gate.

## Why it matters

There's no design-studio surface in the repo today. Iterating on slide composition requires either /pitch (slow) or /investor-portal (gated). A dev-only `/dev/deck` makes per-slide iteration fast (full-bleed, hot reload, no chrome).
