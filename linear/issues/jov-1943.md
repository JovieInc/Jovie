Playwright-based capture for declared design surfaces. Each run produces a baseline (current state) and an after (post-change state). Stores under `agentos/runs/visual-qa/<run_id>/<surface>/{baseline,after}.png`. Surfaces are declared in the Visual QA registry. Distinct scope from [JOV-1605](https://linear.app/jovie/issue/JOV-1605/build-a-screenshot-parity-ledger-for-shell-list-drawer-and-settings) parity ledger and [JOV-1831](https://linear.app/jovie/issue/JOV-1831/new-design-smoke-lane-and-screenshot-parity-cleanup) smoke screenshots — this is the proposal-validation pipeline.
