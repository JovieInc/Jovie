## Symptom

Long-tail polish from the audit — none blocking, but each contributes to the "AI-slop product" smell flagged in `.claude/rules/ui.md`:

* **#57**: Systemic eyebrow-text violation across 11 marketing sites (`marketing-kicker`, `homepage-section-eyebrow`). Banned by default per `.claude/rules/ui.md`.
* **#58**: Inconsistent trailing periods on marketing headlines (`/download`, `/support`)
* **#59**: `/launch` uses Unicode glyphs as icons (`☰ ☷ ✉ ☆ ✓`) — banned per ui rule
* **#60**: Production components render `✓` instead of Lucide `Check` (`ProfileNotificationsMenu.tsx`, `SetupTaskItem.tsx`)
* **#61**: `/about` lists Tory Lanez as credibility credit — brand risk
* **#62**: `/` trust strip "Trusted by artists and teams releasing on" — borderline invented traction claim
* **#71**: `applyStateRewrite` circuit breaker fires at 3 rewrites — fallback UX undocumented
* **#72**: Missing `not-found.tsx` for `/auth`, `/onboarding`, `/app/dashboard`, `/billing` route groups
* **#73**: `setInterval` cleanup verification across 20+ components
* **#74**: Blog hero rendered twice (dead code)
* **#75**: `/ai` `See Pricing` link goes to pricing without Max — resolves with E2 fix
* **#76**: Founder photo `/images/avatars/tim-white-founder.jpg` hardcoded path — verify asset exists
* **#80**: No age gate (13+ per terms; self-attestation only)
* **#81**: No tax/VAT footnote on `/pricing` ("Prices in USD. Tax may apply.")

## Recommended fix

Sweep PRs grouped by area:

1. **Marketing eyebrow sweep** (`#57`): delete `<p className='marketing-kicker'>` and `<p className='homepage-section-eyebrow'>` from all 11 marketing sites; remove or rename the classes to make their use explicit
2. **Headline punctuation sweep** (`#58`): pick one style (Title Case, no trailing period for short headlines), apply across `/download`, `/support`
3. **Unicode glyph → Lucide replacement** (`#59`, `#60`): replace all hardcoded glyphs with Lucide `Menu`, `Inbox`, `Mail`, `Star`, `Check` components
4. **Brand polish** (`#61`, `#62`): founder bio decision (Tory Lanez); trust strip wording change ("Compatible with" / "Works across")
5. **Code quality** (`#71`-`#74`): document state-rewrite breaker behavior; add per-area not-found pages; sweep `setInterval` cleanup; extract `BlogHero`
6. **Misc** (`#80`, `#81`): age gate decision (Linear thread); tax footnote on pricing
