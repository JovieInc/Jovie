## Follow-up

Generate `/press/jovie-brand-kit.zip` at build time, containing every brand asset in `apps/web/public/brand/` plus a top-level `README.md` explaining usage rules. Surface a "Download All · ZIP" CTA at the bottom of `/brand`'s Downloads section that links to it.

Currently the "Need something we haven't shipped?" callout on `/brand` points reviewers to `brand@jov.ie` — that's the v1 stop-gap. A real ZIP is what the page advertises.

## Why it matters

Journalists, partners, and labels expect a one-click "press kit" download. Per-asset downloads work but feel scattered for a logo-wall use case.

## Classification

Required

## Acceptance criteria

* `apps/web/scripts/build-press-kit.mjs` runs in CI (postbuild or `next build` hook) and emits `apps/web/public/press/jovie-brand-kit.zip`.
* ZIP contains: every SVG in `public/brand/`, the PNG favicons, `apple-touch-icon.png`, the new `Jovie-Logo-Mark-{Black,Cream}.svg` + `Jovie-Wordmark-{Black,Cream}.svg` once PR-2 lands, and a generated `BRAND-USAGE.md` summarizing the do/don't rules from `/brand`.
* `/brand` Downloads section reveals a "Download All · ZIP" button.
* `/press` and `/press-kit` redirects still resolve to `/brand#downloads` (already in `next.config.js`).
