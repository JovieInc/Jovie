## Duplicate systems involved

* remaining raw `/api` fetch calls in components, hooks, and libs after phase-specific migrations
* bespoke response parsing, retry assumptions, and local loading/error state handling

## Shared target

Complete the transport cleanup by migrating or explicitly exempting every remaining raw `/api` fetch site.

## User and engineering impact

This is the residual cleanup that keeps the transport policy from regressing. It also captures the remaining performance wins from cancellation, caching, and consistent background refresh behavior.

## Representative evidence

The current audit found \~37 raw `/api` fetch sites before any phase-specific cleanup. This issue is the final sweep after the higher-leverage work is complete.
