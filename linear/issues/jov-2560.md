Implement the Jovie Merch MVP from the approved plan.

Scope:

* Jovie-owned merch card source of truth
* Chat-driven merch generation and selection
* Printful catalog/mockup/order fulfillment integration
* Stripe platform checkout without Connect for MVP
* Internal artist payout ledger and manual payout states
* Public profile merch card and product checkout route
* Stripe and Printful webhooks plus fulfillment worker
* Focused tests, docs, PR, and landing

Acceptance:

* Artists can generate exactly 3 merch options from chat, select one, and publish a real merch card.
* Fans can select a variant and pay through Stripe Checkout.
* Paid orders enqueue Printful fulfillment idempotently and ledger artist payout liability.
* Public profile never shows fake merch when no live merch record exists.
* MVP does not represent artist payouts as automatic.
