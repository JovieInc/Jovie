## Follow-up

Enable `eslint-plugin-react-hooks` `exhaustive-deps` rule at error level. Catches stale-closure bugs (object/function deps that recreate every render) — root cause of bug class #3 (state flicker during async churn).

The drawer flicker bug in PR B is exactly the kind of bug this rule could have surfaced — the effect at `ProfileUnifiedDrawer.tsx:312-319` had a non-obvious data-derived dep.

## Why it matters

Stale-closure useEffect bugs are a top contributor to "human-eye-only" flicker/state bugs. The rule has a high false-positive rate at rollout, which is why it's deferred.
