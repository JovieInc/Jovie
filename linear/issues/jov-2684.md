## Goal

Create a distinctive, high-quality voice for Summer — the AI Chief of Staff character.

## Character Brief

Summer is:

* An AI Chief of Staff (materialized as a voice/interface)
* Professional, efficient, direct — no fluff
* Proactive and strategic
* Female-presenting
* Sounds like a COO who happens to be an AI: calm, authoritative, warm but business-first
* Think: a world-class executive assistant who runs your entire operation
* NOT robotic or monotone — intelligent and engaging
* Energy: composed, confident, slightly ahead of everyone else in the room

## Approach

Since Summer is fictional, we cannot clone from real audio. Options:

### Option A: Curate an existing ElevenLabs voice

1. Browse ElevenLabs voice library for voices matching the character
2. Test top 3-5 candidates with demo scripts
3. Fine-tune voice settings (stability, similarity, style)
4. Save the voice_id

### Option B: Voice design via ElevenLabs Speech Synthesis

1. Use a base voice close to target
2. Adjust voice_settings to match character
3. Generate demos and iterate

## Demo Scripts to Generate (for each candidate voice)

1. Status update: "Tim, here's your briefing. Four crons running, two errors resolved. Open PR on Jovie needs your review. Inbox has three investors waiting for follow-up."
2. Morning briefing: "Good morning, Tim. Here's what matters today. Three things need your decision. Everything else is handled."
3. Escalation: "Tim, this needs your call. I've laid out the options and my recommendation. You're the only one who can decide this."
4. Casual check-in: "Quick update — the content research agent found 30 signals across Reddit and Twitter. The top pain point is still streaming payouts. I've updated the idea queue."

## Deliverables

* Selected voice_id saved to \~/youtube-training-data/summer_voice.json
* Demo audio files (at least 4, one per script above)
* Voice settings documented (model, stability, similarity, style, speaker_boost)
* Playback so Tim can hear and iterate
