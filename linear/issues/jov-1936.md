HUD UI at `/admin/ops` to review pending design proposals. Three actions per proposal: yes (approve and dispatch), no (reject and store direction in taste memory), yes-with-notes (approve with amendment notes injected into the dispatch payload). On decision: update the Linear issue status, write the outcome to `agentos/memory/design-taste.md`, and trigger the dispatch job (D5) for approved proposals.
