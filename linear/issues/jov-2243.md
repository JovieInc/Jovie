## Follow-up

v1 schema (`connector_accounts` unique on `(userId, provider, providerAccountId)`) supports multiple Google accounts per user. v1 UI does NOT — Connect Google connects exactly one account. When DJ has work@ and personal@ Gmail with bookings in both, they can't connect both.

When picking up: design the multi-account flow. /settings/connectors shows a list of connected accounts per provider; "Add another Gmail" button starts a fresh OAuth with `prompt=consent` to force account picker; SuggestedActions show which account they came from.

## Why it matters

Working DJs commonly have a personal Gmail (where venues actually email) plus an artist-management work Gmail. Forcing them to pick one cuts the signal in half.

## Classification

Candidate

Pickup agent must first judge whether to implement, close, or split this. Trigger: beta-user feedback asks for multi-account or DJ has a known multi-account need.
