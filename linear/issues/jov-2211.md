# JOV-2211: [P1][Demo] Build campaign-aware fan notification scheduling

- **Status:** Done
- **Priority:** None
- **Assignee:** Tim White
- **Team:** JOV
- **Project:** —
- **Labels:** codex
- **URL:** https://linear.app/jovie/issue/JOV-2211/p1demo-build-campaign-aware-fan-notification-scheduling
- **Updated:** 2026-05-31T06:39:55.979Z

## Evidence

* Screenshot/frame: 90-second founder demo on /demovideo
* Route: /demovideo
* Observed behavior: The demo shows Jovie drafting and scheduling a fan notification for the weekend drop.
* Expected behavior: Fan notifications should be generated from campaign context, scoped to a selected segment, scheduled for launch, and previewable before send.

Shown cinematically in PR [JovieInc/Jovie#8671](https://linear.app/jovie/review/fixdemo-stabilize-public-demo-video-embed-82094abf613c); production implementation needed.

## Impact

Notifications are a key front-data-layer wedge. Campaigns need contextual product notifications, not generic email blasts.

## Likely root cause

Notification drafting and scheduling are not yet campaign-aware across segments, smart links, and launch windows.

## Acceptance criteria

* Notification drafts include campaign, segment, copy, destination, and launch time.\\n- Artists can preview mobile/fan-facing copy.\\n- Scheduling respects segment eligibility and unsubscribe state.\\n- Sends are idempotent and observable.

## Test plan

* Unit tests for eligibility and unsubscribe filtering.\\n- Integration test for campaign to scheduled notification.\\n- Manual notification preview check.\\n- Console/network checks on campaign approval flow.

## Suggested fix

Extend notification scheduling with campaign context, segment targeting, preview payloads, and send-state monitoring.
