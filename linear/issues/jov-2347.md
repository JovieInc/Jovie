[JOV-2338](https://linear.app/jovie/issue/JOV-2338/jove-pr3-memory-store-deep-research-fetcher-context-builder-extension) ships memory recall as recency + kind filter. v2 replaces it with embedding-based semantic search: embed each `jove_memory_entries.content` on insert, search via pgvector at recall time. Returns the most semantically-relevant entries for the proposer's current context, not just the most recent.

## Why it matters

Recency loses utility as the memory grows. A creator with 6 months of Jove use will have hundreds of entries; recency alone surfaces noise. Embedding search keeps the proposer grounded in the right past context regardless of when it was captured.
