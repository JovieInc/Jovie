## Follow-up

DESIGN_V1 and its 8 aliases are now permanently `true` (PR #8406). The mechanical follow-up is to walk every consumer, delete the `false` branch, and inline the `true` branch. Rough scope: \~30+ usage sites per the plan.

After this lands, the flag entries themselves can be deleted from `APP_FLAG_DEFAULTS`, `APP_FLAG_KEYS`, `APP_FLAG_OVERRIDE_KEYS`, `APP_FLAG_DESCRIPTIONS`, `LOCAL_DEFAULT_ONLY_FLAGS`, `APP_FLAG_REGISTRY`, and `DESIGN_V1_ALIAS_FLAGS` — at which point `LEGACY_STATSIG_GATE_KEYS` entries for DESIGN_V1 surfaces can also be removed, plus the `feature-flags-registry.test.ts` invariant introduced in PR #8406.

Recommend split into two PRs:

* 4a: inline branches in product code (high-churn, low-risk).
* 4b: delete the registry entries + invariant test once 4a is green.

## Why it matters

* Reduces flag surface area (registry, tests, override store, DevToolbar).
* Removes the localStorage-override risk (`code:DESIGN_V1`) entirely.
* Makes future design refactors cheaper because there is no dead "old" branch to maintain.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Zero `useAppFlag('DESIGN_V1' …)` / `useFeatureFlag('DESIGN_V1' …)` / `getAppFlagValue('DESIGN_V1' …)` callsites remain (and same for the 8 aliases).
- [ ] The 9 entries removed from contracts.
- [ ] `flag-registration-guardrail.test.ts` and the rewritten DESIGN_V1 invariant in `feature-flags-registry.test.ts` updated to match.
- [ ] Typecheck + full unit test suite green.
- [ ] Workflow `design-v1` suite filter cleanup in `.github/workflows/nightly-tests.yml` (orphaned conditions left after PR #8406's canary deletion) — drop `- design-v1` from `inputs.suite` enum and remove the `suite == 'design-v1'` branches that no longer gate any unique step.

## Dependency

PR #8406 must land first (already merged). 4a blocks 4b.
