## Follow-up

After v1 ships (calendar entry on approve), v1.1 extends `suggested_actions.sideEffects[]` to also write the tour page update + fire fan notification when the DJ approves a missing event. Schema is ready in v1: `suggested_actions.kind` enum will accept `tour_page.update`, `fan_notification.send`; `sideEffects` jsonb column exists from day 1.

## Why it matters

CEO review identified this as Jovie's defensible wedge vs. Motion / Superhuman / Gemini-in-Gmail. Private calendar suggestions overlap directly with commodity AI. Fan-facing termination is the unique Jovie capability — touring DJs' fans care about new shows being announced.

## Classification

Required

## Acceptance criteria

* DJ approves a "missing show" `SuggestedActionCard` once.
* Side effects: (a) Google Calendar event created; (b) `tourDates` row inserted with `confirmationStatus='confirmed'`; (c) public tour page revalidated; (d) fan notification fan-out enqueued (subject to existing `tourDateNotificationPrefs`).
* Approval card preview block shows all four slots BEFORE approval ("This will create: 1 calendar event, 1 tour-page entry, \~N fan notifications").
* All side effects are idempotent via the same `idempotencyKey`.
